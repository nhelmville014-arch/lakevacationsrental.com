The CASAMIRA Philosophy

Wellness by the Water

We believe the lake is the best therapist. CASAMIRA is designed around the restorative power of nature — slow mornings, gentle movement, family time and genuine rest.

🌅

Morning Stillness

Wake to the sound of water, coffee in hand, lake view through the kitchen windows. No agenda. Bashan Lake rewards those who simply sit and listen.

🛶

Mindful Movement

Kayak at your own pace. Paddleboard with a sunrise behind you. Wade in clean, clear water. Movement out here feels nothing like the gym — it feels like play.

🌿

Nature Immersion

Hawks overhead. Deer & Red Fox at dusk. Stars without light pollution. The property's many secluded acres invite you to disconnect from screens and reconnect with what matters.

🍽️

Nourish Together

Gather around a lakeside or lawn grill. Cook real meals in a fully stocked kitchen. Eat with the people you love at picnic tables overlooking the water. Simple, healing joy.

😴

Deep Rest

Fresh air, clean water, and quiet nights make sleep different here. Newly renovated bedrooms, comfortable beds, and no car traffic noise — just the sounds of nature and fun at the lake.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Healing

Unplug together. Swim together. Paddle board together. Hang out at the beach together. Some of the best family moments happen when there's nowhere to be and a whole lake in front of you.
